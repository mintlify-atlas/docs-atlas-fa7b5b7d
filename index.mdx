## What is Sassamaru BR-25?

Sassamaru BR-25 is a statistical football prediction system built specifically for the **Campeonato Brasileiro**. It combines two well-established rating methodologies:

- **Poisson distribution** — estimates the expected number of goals for each team based on historical attack/defense strength relative to the league average.
- **Elo rating system** — tracks team strength dynamically, updating after each match based on results and goal margin.

Together, they produce accurate, calibrated probability distributions for every match: probability of a home win, draw, or away win — along with expected goal totals for each team.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Load historical match data">
    The model reads `br-25.csv` — a CSV of past Brasileirão matches with home team, away team, and final scores.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Calculate Poisson attack/defense strengths">
    Each team's attacking and defensive strength is computed relative to the league average, separately for home and away contexts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build dynamic Elo ratings">
    Elo ratings start at 1500 and are updated chronologically through every historical match. Goal margin affects the K-factor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Predict match outcomes">
    For any fixture, the hybrid model combines Poisson-estimated goals with Elo-adjusted factors to compute win/draw/loss probabilities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Output results">
    Results are printed as a Markdown table ready for reports, or saved to a timestamped `.md` file from the GUI.
  </Step>
</Steps>
